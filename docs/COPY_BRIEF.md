# Copy Brief

## Tone
- Warm
- Professional
- Human
- Trustworthy
- Calm
- Reassuring
- Clear
- Conversion-aware

## Avoid
- Generic healthcare cliches
- Corporate fluff
- Empty buzzwords
- Overly salesy language
- Dense paragraphs
- Unclear service descriptions

## Emphasise
- Trust
- Professionalism
- Ease of access
- Personalised support
- Smaller client load / higher care quality
- Fast and simple enquiry pathways
- Confidence for families and referrers

## Core Positioning
Gentle Care Nursing provides personalised, high-quality in-home nursing and care services with a strong focus on dedicated support, trust, and making it easy for families and referral partners to get help quickly.

## Messaging Strategy
The copy must:
- reduce confusion
- build trust quickly
- make the business feel professional and safe
- remove friction from making contact
- clearly explain services
- reassure families and referral partners
- support SEO intent naturally

## CRO Copy Principles
Apply principles inspired by:
- Alex Hormozi: clarity, offer strength, risk reduction, obvious value
- Sabri Suby: direct response structure, compelling CTAs, conversion flow
- ThrillX / Arsh Sanwarwala: message hierarchy, strategic page structure, data-driven conversion layout
- Caleb Ulku: search intent alignment, keyword-aware page structure, local SEO relevance

## Core CTAs
- Request Care
- Make a Referral
- Speak With Our AI Assistant
- Book a Consultation
- Contact Us

## Homepage Messaging Priorities
1. Who we help
2. What services we provide
3. Why trust Gentle Care
4. What makes them different
5. What the user should do next

## UX Writing Principles
- Use short, readable sections
- Use simple headings
- Reduce cognitive load
- Make buttons action-oriented
- Keep forms clear and easy
- Make every section support the next action

# Demo talking points for Jemma — video walkthrough

Use this alongside the preview link when recording. Keeps the walkthrough tight and value-focused so Jemma sees what’s been built and why the next $2k continues the same standard.

---

## 1. Opening (30 seconds)

- "Jemma, this is a quick walkthrough of what's live on your preview site right now — the foundation we've built so you can see where your deposit has gone and what we're building on next."
- "Everything you're about to see is already live on the review link; nothing is mock-up."

---

## 2. Brand and first impression (1–2 min)

**Show:** Homepage load, then scroll slowly through hero and first section.

**Say:**

- "First thing we did was lock in **your** brand. The colours are the exact hexes from your current site and Instagram — the berry, the blush, the greys — so it feels like Gentle Care, not a template."
- "The hero is no longer flush to the edges. We brought the content in with a max width and more padding so it feels calmer and more premium — the kind of first impression that matches the care you deliver."
- "There's a subtle entrance: the headline and photo ease in when the page loads. It's small, but it makes the site feel considered and professional."

**Value line:**  
"This is the level of polish you'd usually see on sites that cost $20–40k; we're building it in from the start."

---

## 3. Trust and credibility (1 min)

**Show:** Trust bar (NDIS / DVA pills) and maybe one "why us" or process section.

**Say:**

- "Your trust badges — Registered NDIS Provider, Registered DVA — are now on a white pill with dark text so they're readable and clear. They're the first credibility hit after the hero."
- "As you scroll, sections reveal in gently so the page doesn't feel static. Same treatment on the Services page and key inner pages — consistent, premium feel."

**Value line:**  
"We're making it obvious you're registered and professional without shouting; that's what converts families and referrers."

---

## 4. Navigation and structure (1 min)

**Show:** Header nav, one dropdown (e.g. Services), then scroll to 1–2 sections (e.g. Who we help, How it works).

**Say:**

- "Navigation is set up for how people look for you: About, Services with sub-pages, Referrals, Contact, plus the phone number and Request Care button always visible."
- "Sections are clearly separated by background tone — cream, blush, white — so it's easy to see you're in a new 'chapter' without hard lines. We refined this so it feels like one continuous, premium page."

**Value line:**  
"Structure and clarity matter for both users and Google; we've built that in."

---

## 5. Mobile and "little details" (1 min)

**Show:** Resize to mobile or use dev tools device view. Scroll to footer, tap a social icon, then scroll back up and trigger scroll-to-top.

**Say:**

- "On mobile, the same content and hierarchy — nothing important is hidden. The sticky Request Care bar appears once they scroll past the hero, and it hides when they reach the footer so it's not in the way."
- "When they're in the footer, a scroll-to-top button appears so they can jump back up without scrolling. On desktop, that button appears whenever they've scrolled down. Small detail, but it shows we've thought about how people actually use the site."
- "Social links in the footer go to your real Facebook and Instagram and open in a new tab."

**Value line:**  
"These are the kinds of details that make a site feel finished and professional, not rushed."

---

## 6. What's live right now (30 sec)

**Say (no need to click every page):**

- "Homepage is fully built with your copy, your flow, and your brand. Services hub, NDIS, DVA, aged care, private nursing, post-hospital care, complex care, areas we serve, referrers page, contact, FAQ — all live and wired into the same design system and structure."
- "So you're not looking at a single landing page; you're looking at the core of a full site that's already live and consistent."

---

## 7. What the $2k has delivered so far (30 sec)

**Say:**

- "So far your deposit has bought: a custom design system matched to your brand, a responsive site with clear sectioning and animations, multiple service and info pages, trust and conversion elements (CTAs, reassurance copy, scroll behaviour), and a base that's ready for the next phase."
- "The next $2,000 is where we plug in the engine: your GoHighLevel sub-account, forms and enquiries feeding into your workflows, the chat widget with voice and AI, and the automation that turns enquiries into booked clients without you chasing leads manually."

---

## 8. Tease what's next (30 sec)

**Say:**

- "Coming next: the chat widget bottom-right with a voice agent and an AI conversational bot so when someone asks a question, they get an instant, on-brand response. Plus the review side — we'll run a Google review campaign and an AI review agent that responds in your voice to every new review. That's the layer that turns the site into a lead and reputation machine."
- "So this recording is the 'before' — the foundation. The next phase is where it starts working for you 24/7."

---

## 9. Closing

- "Have a click around the preview link; everything we walked through is there. When you're ready, we'll lock in the second $2,000 and move straight into the integrations and AI components. Thanks, Jemma."

## 2. NDIS Provider Finder — needs verification (5 min)

**What it is:** the official tool the NDIS uses for participants and coordinators to find registered providers. Most NDIS-funded queries route here, including from AI search.

**What to do — three checks:**

**Check 1 — listing exists.** Open this URL in your phone's browser:

> https://www.ndis.gov.au/participants/working-providers/find-registered-provider/provider-finder

In the search box, type **"Gentle Care Nursing"** and search. You should see Gentle Care Nursing Services Pty Ltd appear. **If it doesn't appear**, that's the highest-priority fix on this whole list — log into your NDIS provider portal at https://providers.ndiscommission.gov.au and confirm registration is current.

**Check 2 — Sydney appears as service area.** Click into the Gentle Care result. Confirm:
- Service area shows Sydney (or specific Sydney regions)
- Phone is 1300 004 267
- Address is North Strathfield NSW 2137

If any of those don't match, there's an "update provider profile" link in the NDIS Commission portal — fix the mismatch there. (Mismatched details are the #1 reason providers stop appearing in Provider Finder results.)

**Check 3 — registration groups.** While you're in the provider portal, confirm the **registration groups** (the official categories your registration covers) include the ones we claim on the website:
- Assistance with Daily Life
- Community Nursing Care
- High Intensity Daily Personal Activities
- Community Participation

If any of those four are missing from your registration but we say we offer them on the website, either we need to update the registration scope (with the NDIS Commission) or update the website. **Email me what registration groups you're approved for** and I'll align the website wording to match exactly.

**Bookmark:** https://providers.ndiscommission.gov.au — your NDIS Commission login. Worth keeping in your password manager.

## 3. My Aged Care provider directory — needs verification (10 min)

**What it is:** the consumer-facing directory at https://www.myagedcare.gov.au/find-a-provider that families use to find aged-care home services. Discharge planners and AI engines pull from it heavily for "aged care at home Sydney" and "Support at Home" queries.

**What to do — two checks:**

**Check 1 — public-facing listing.** Open the consumer search:

> https://www.myagedcare.gov.au/find-a-provider

Search for **"Gentle Care"** or filter by **postcode 2137** (your office) and service type **"Nursing"** or **"Personal care"**. Confirm Gentle Care appears. Note exactly what categories appear next to your name (e.g. Support at Home, CHSP, Home Care Package, etc.).

**Check 2 — the categories match what we say on the site.** Our `/aged-care` page says we accept:
- Support at Home
- Commonwealth Home Support Programme (CHSP)
- DVA
- Private fee-for-service

If My Aged Care lists you under any *other* category, we should add that to the website. If My Aged Care is missing one we claim, we need to either update your provider profile in My Aged Care or pull that claim from the site so they match.

**If Gentle Care doesn't appear at all in My Aged Care's directory:** this is fixable. Log in to https://my.gov.au/ → Aged Care section, and check the provider profile. Government-funded aged-care providers self-manage their listing; updates flow through within 24-48 hours.

**Worth knowing:** Support at Home replaced Home Care Packages on **1 November 2025**. Our `/aged-care` page already references Support at Home correctly. Your My Aged Care provider profile should also use the new programme name — if it still says "Home Care Package" only, that's an outdated profile and should be refreshed.

**Bookmark:** https://my.gov.au/ → Aged Care provider portal.

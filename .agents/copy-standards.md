# Munyal Brand Voice & Copy Standards

## 🇦🇺 Regional Standard: Australian English
All copy **MUST** use Australian English. Never use American English.

### Key Spelling Rules:
- **-ise over -ize**: *optimise, realise, prioritise* (NOT optimize, realize, prioritize).
- **-our over -or**: *colour, favour, labour* (NOT color, favor, labor).
- **-re over -er**: *centre, theatre* (NOT center, theater).
- **Double 'l' in inflections**: *travelling, cancelled* (NOT traveling, canceled).
- **Specific terms**: *suburb* (not neighborhood), *mobile* (not cell phone), *booking* (not reservation).

## 🗣️ Brand Voice: Human-to-Human (H2H)
Munyal speaks like a partner, not a corporation. The tone is **authentic, genuine, and grounded.**

### The "No-BS" Filter:
- **NO Salesy Fluff**: Avoid words like "synergy," "cutting-edge," "revolutionary," or "game-changing."
- **NO Corporate Jargon**: Avoid "leverage," "bandwidth," "scaleable architecture" (unless talking technically to a tech person, but even then, keep it simple).
- **YES Plain English**: Say "make more money" instead of "optimise revenue streams." Say "save time" instead of "increase operational efficiency."

### Authentic Connection:
- Speak directly to the business owner (Minas and Sandra types).
- Use "we" and "you."
- Acknowledge the grind: "We know you're busy at the grooming table," "Stop answering calls at 9 PM."
- Focus on the **result**, not the **feature**.

## 🚀 Converting Components (Inspired by 21st-Day/Modern SaaS)
- **Content-First Hero**: Use a headline that hits the primary emotion (Freedom/Wealth).
- **The "Before/After" Proof**: Visualise the transformation from "Analog Chaos" to "Munyal Clarity."
- **Frictionless CTAs**: "Claim Your Suburb" is strong. Keep it.
- **Micro-Copy**: Use tooltips or small labels to explain *why* something matters (Australian English).

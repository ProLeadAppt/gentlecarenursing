Key Design Improvements

Typography Hierarchy

Break headline into 3 visual weights — "Compassionate" (large, dark), "In-Home Care" (large, berry), "& Disability Support" (medium, italic, muted). Creates rhythm and draws eye to key phrase.

Ambient Depth

Subtle radial gradient orbs (sage, teal) drift slowly behind content. Creates living, breathing background without overwhelming. GPU-composited for 60fps.

CTA Refinement

Primary button gets gradient + shadow for depth. Secondary becomes inline text "or call [number]" — reduces decision paralysis and adds human touch.

Social Proof Integration

Floating badges (24h response, 5.0 rating) overlay the hero image — proof is visible at first glance, not buried below. Pulsing green dot on response badge implies "always on".

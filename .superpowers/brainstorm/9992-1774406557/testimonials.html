Animation Choreography

Staggered reveal sequence
Enter view
Section header fades up (eyebrow → title, 0.2s stagger)
+0.3s
Card 1 rises: Translates from y:40 → y:0 with opacity 0→1. Top accent bar slides in from left. Quote text fades in 0.15s after card. Spring easing (stiffness: 100, damping: 20).
+0.5s
Card 2 rises: Same animation, slightly higher final position (translateY: -4px — elevated "featured" feel). Accent bar uses teal gradient.
+0.7s
Card 3 rises: Same spring animation. On completion, all 3 cards have subtle hover states enabled (lift + shadow increase on mouseover).
+1.0s
Stars in each card fill left→right (gold cascade, 0.05s per star). Attribution rows fade in simultaneously.

Key Design Improvements

Colour-Coded by Audience

Each testimonial card has a unique accent colour — sage for families, teal for coordinators, berry for hospital professionals. Subtly signals "we serve all your audiences" without saying it.

Elevated Middle Card

Centre card sits 4px higher with a deeper shadow. Creates visual depth and draws the eye to the most relevant testimonial (coordinator — the primary conversion audience).

Richer Quotes

Longer, more emotionally resonant quotes in serif (Cormorant). "I finally felt like I could breathe" > "They treated Mum like family". The extra sentence creates the emotional hook.

Location Context

Add suburb/area to each attribution ("Sydney", "Inner West", "RPA"). Localisation builds trust — visitors think "they serve my area" and it reinforces GEO SEO signals.
